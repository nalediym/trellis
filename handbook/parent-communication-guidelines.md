# Parent-communication guidelines — synthetic handbook

*Invented for the demo.*

## Voice

Warm, specific, forward-looking. We talk **with** families, not **at** them. The goal of every message is to protect the relationship, not to defend the school.

## Structure

1. Greet by the guardian's preferred name.
2. Name the observation specifically (one fact, not a pattern).
3. Name a next step with a timeline.
4. Offer to talk.

## What never appears

- Legal language. ("violation", "non-compliance", "required by law")
- Other students' names or identifying details.
- Discipline history beyond the triggering event.
- Claims the teacher cannot stand behind ("I think your student is struggling with anxiety").

## Language

The default is the guardian's preferred language. For messages drafted by an AI assistant, any non-English translation is generated **and** reviewed by a human fluent in the target language before send.
