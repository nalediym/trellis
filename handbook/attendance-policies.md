# Attendance policies — synthetic handbook

*This document is a fabrication for a portfolio demo. All policy numbers, contact methods, and escalation thresholds are invented and do not reflect any real school.*

## Daily attendance

Teachers take attendance in the first 10 minutes of homeroom. Late marks are applied at minute 11. A full absence is recorded if the student has not checked in by the end of period 2.

## Escalation thresholds

- **1 unexplained absence in a rolling 10-school-day window** — no communication; record only.
- **2 unexplained absences** — the homeroom teacher sends a templated check-in to the guardian within 48 hours.
- **3 unexplained absences** — a voice call by the dean of students; the dean logs the call in the SIS.
- **5 unexplained absences in a rolling 20-day window** — a meeting is scheduled.

## Contact preferences

Guardian records contain a preferred method (text, email, or voice) and a preferred language. Initial contact uses the preferred method. Second contact escalates to voice.

## When not to contact

Do not contact guardians between 9pm and 7am local time except in safety emergencies. Do not include disciplinary detail in attendance outreach — that is a separate workflow with a separate blueprint.
